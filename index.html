About ReportTag

ReportTag was set up as a portal aimed specifically at the polar tourism industry in an attempt to collect images of animal tags. However photo submittions are welcome from anyone. We will share the data that comes from your photos with collaborating scientists.

Contact us

You can contact ReportTag at admin@ReportTag.com. If you have any questions, just send us an email.
